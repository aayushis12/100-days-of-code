Linus Torvalds

Talk is cheap. Show me the code

Linus's Life History

  • Dec 1969 : Born in Helsinki, Finland to Anna and Nils Torvalds
  • Jan 1988 : Joined University of Helsinki
  • Aug 1991 : Purchased the Intel 80386-based IBM PC with Minix OS. With this PC, he began to work and research on the Linux Operating System.
  • Sep 1991 : Started a personal project to create a new free OS
  • Oct 1991 : Richard Stallman expresses interest in having the Free Software Foundation distribute a GNU system with the Linux kernel.
  • Mar 1994 : Torvalds judges all components of the kernel to be fully matured and released version 1.0 of Linux
  • May 1996 : Made TUX as the official mascot for Linux
  • Aug 1998 : Linus made into the cover of Forbes
  • May 2001 : Published his autobiography entitled Just For Fun
  • Aug 2005 : Created GIT

Awards and achievements

  • 2014 : IEEE Computer Pioneer Award
  • 2012 : Internet Hall of Fame
  • 2012 : Millennium Technology Prize
  • 2008 : Hall of Fellows
  • 1996 : Asteroid 9793 Torvalds named after him.
